Adult Ministry

We offer a Men's Bible Study that meets every Monday night at 7:00 PM in the Family Life Building, led by Rev. Derek Greene. Additionally, there is a Men's Prayer Breakfast Group that meets every other Tuesday at 8:00 AM at the Depot in Lattimore. Our Senior Adults, known as the 'Pleasanteers,' enjoy regular outings and also hold a Bible Study on Tuesday mornings at the church.

Youth Ministry

The Youth meet weekly on Sunday mornings and Wednesday evenings. We offer a Youth Sunday School class and Wednesday night studies, which include topical studies and in depth studies of various books of the Bible. Throughout the year, students also have the opportunity to participate in local and regional events and retreats.

Children's Ministry

The children meet weekly on Sunday mornings and Wednesday evenings. We offer a nursery, Sunday School classes, Children's Church, Wednesday night studies, a children's choir, VBS, and other occasional activities and outings for both our children and families.
