Sunday and Wednesday Worship

Our usual Wednesday night activities include Children, Youth, and Adult bible studies at 6:30PM, and choir practice at 7:30.
Sunday morning we have Sunday School at 9:00 and Worship at 10:00.

Children's Ministry

The children meet weekly on Sunday mornings and Wednesday evenings. We offer a nursery, Children's Church, a children's choir, VBS, and other occasional activities and outings for both our children and families.

Youth Ministry

The Youth meet weekly on Sunday mornings and Wednesday evenings engaging in topical studies and in depth studies of various books of the Bible. Throughout the year, students also have the opportunity to participate in local and regional events and retreats.
